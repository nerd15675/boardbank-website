Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

BoardBank is designed to work without requiring you to create an account.

We do not collect personal information such as your name, location, contacts, financial information, or camera images. BoardBank may request access to your device camera only when you use QR scanning features.

Game information such as player names, balances and saved games is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

2. How We Use Information

Any information entered into BoardBank is used only to provide app features, such as managing players and board game balances.

3. Saved Games

Saved games are stored locally on your device. BoardBank does not have access to your saved games and cannot recover deleted data.

4. NFC Support

BoardBank does not currently support NFC cards. However, NFC functionality may be added in a future update.

If NFC features are introduced, they will only be used to improve gameplay and identify players within the app.

QR Code Scanning

BoardBank may use your device camera to scan QR codes for features within the app.

Camera access is only used while you are actively using the QR scanning feature. BoardBank does not record, store, or upload images or videos from your camera.

Any information contained within a QR code is processed only to provide the requested app functionality.

5. Support Requests

If you contact BoardBank Support, we may receive information that you choose to provide, such as your email address and message.

Support information is only used to respond to your request.

Email:
boardbank.support@gmail.com

6. Third-Party Services

BoardBank does not currently use advertising, tracking, or analytics services.

If additional services are added in future updates, this privacy policy will be updated.

7. Children's Privacy

BoardBank is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as BoardBank develops. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact:

boardbank.support@gmail.com
